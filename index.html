Crystal Collector

Four crystals are diplayed below. Their values have been randomly generated, but will remain constant for the duration of each round.

Click on the crystals to increase your current value. If you can match your value to the target value, you win the round. If at any point your value exceeds the target value, you lose the round. In either event, all values will regenerate at the start of each round.
